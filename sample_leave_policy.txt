Company Leave Policy (Sample Document for Testing)

Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year,
accrued monthly. Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days per year,
with a doctor's note required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward into the next year up to a
maximum of 5 days; any leave beyond that is forfeited on December 31st.

Employees must submit leave requests through the HR portal at least
3 working days in advance, except in documented emergencies.

Maternity leave is 26 weeks paid, and paternity leave is 2 weeks paid,
both requiring at least 30 days advance notice where possible.
